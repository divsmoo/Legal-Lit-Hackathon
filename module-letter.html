Draft a Legal Letter

Review an AI-generated tenancy termination letter and find the issue.

Prompt used

Draft a tenancy termination letter for a residential lease in Singapore. 
Cite any relevant statute or clause if needed. Avoid including personal identifiers.

AI-Generated Letter (mock)

Dear Sir/Madam,

This letter provides notice of termination of the Tenancy Agreement for 12 Sample Avenue, Singapore 123456.
As per the law, the tenancy will end on 30 November 2025.

The landlord guarantees the return of the full security deposit within 7 days.
Please contact the undersigned at john.doe@example.com for coordination.

Sincerely,
Landlord
Need a hint? Look for vague legal references, absolute language, and potential PII.

        • Avoid absolute promises (“guarantees”); prefer “subject to inspection / applicable law”.
