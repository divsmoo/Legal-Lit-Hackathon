Summarise a Judgment

Does the AI summary show sufficient authority and currency?

Prompt used

Summarise the key holdings of a recent Singapore Court of Appeal decision on landlord-tenant disputes.
Provide the case name, citation, and the year of decision.

AI-Generated Summary (mock)

The court decided that landlords can always keep the full deposit when tenants leave early.
The judgment is authoritative as per the law.
Need a hint? Look for missing case name/citation and over-broad claims (“always”).

        Learning points

        • Always include case name, neutral citation, and year for summaries.
        • Avoid absolute statements; outcomes depend on facts and law.
        • State jurisdiction and date (e.g., “Singapore law, as at Sep 2025”).
